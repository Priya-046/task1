Early Life

Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu is an Indian actor, producer and philanthropist who works in Telugu cinema. He is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema was born on 9 August 1975 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He is the son of famous Telugu actor Krishna and Indira Devi.He is the fourth of five siblings, after Ramesh Babu, Padmavathi, and Manjula, and before Priyadarsini. His family hails from Burripalem in Guntur district,Andhra Pradesh.[15] Mahesh Babu spent most of his childhood in Chennai and completed his schooling at St. Bede's Anglo Indian Higher Secondary School.

Later, he completed his Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) degree from Loyola College in Chennai. Since his father was a famous actor, Mahesh Babu was introduced to the film industry at a young age. He acted in several films as a child artist before becoming a lead actor in Telugu cinema.

Career

At the age of four, Babu visited the sets of the Telugu film Needa (1979) where its director Dasari Narayana Rao shot a few sequences of his as a part of the narrative in the presence of the former's brother Ramesh. Mahesh Babu started his acting career as a child artist in the movie "Needa" in 1979. He later appeared in movies such as Poratam, Shankharavam, and Koduku Diddina Kapuram. After completing his education, he made his debut as a lead actor in the movie "Rajakumarudu" in 1999. The film was directed by K. Raghavendra Rao and became a commercial success.

Mahesh Babu gained massive popularity with the blockbuster movie "Okkadu" in 2003. His movie "Pokiri" in 2006 became one of the biggest hits in Telugu cinema and established him as a superstar. He later acted in several successful films such as Dookudu, Khaleja, Businessman, and 1: Nenokkadine. Because of his natural acting and stylish performance, he is considered one of the biggest stars in the Telugu film industry.

Personal Life

Mahesh Babu married former actress Namrata Shirodkar in the year 2005. The couple has two children: Gautam Ghattamaneni and Sitara Ghattamaneni. Mahesh Babu is known for being very close to his family and often spends his free time with them despite his busy film schedule.

Apart from acting, Mahesh Babu is also a successful entrepreneur and brand ambassador. He has endorsed several major brands including Thums Up, Navratna Oil, Mahindra Tractors, and Reliance Trends.

Social Work

Mahesh Babu is also known for his philanthropic activities. He supports many social initiatives related to education and healthcare. He adopted the villages Burripalem in Andhra Pradesh and Siddhapuram in Telangana to improve infrastructure, education, and medical facilities.

Through his charitable foundation, Mahesh Babu also provides financial support for children who need heart surgeries and medical treatments. Mahesh Babu's philanthropic initiatives have facilitated over 5,000 successful heart surgeries for children. In partnership with Andhra Hospitals, his foundation provides free, life-saving cardiac procedures for children from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.His contributions to society have made him one of the most respected actors in the Indian film industry.
